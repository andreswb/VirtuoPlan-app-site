Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a practice plan?

Go to the Practice tab, tap "Create," select your instrument, set your weekly goal and practice days, allocate time to different practice types, and choose the plan duration. The app will automatically generate practice sessions for you.

Can I track multiple instruments?

Yes! Add all your instruments in the Instruments tab. Each instrument tracks its own practice time and can have separate practice plans.

How does the metronome work?

The metronome features Italian tempo markings, tap tempo functionality, and 11 different time signatures. Adjust the BPM with the slider or by tapping, select your time signature, and tap play to start.

What tuning standards are available?

The tuner supports A=440, 442, 443, and 444 Hz tuning standards. You can also enable transposition for B‚ô≠, E‚ô≠, F, and A instruments to see note names adjusted for your instrument.

How do I change the language?

Go to Settings (gear icon in the Learn tab), tap Language, and select your preferred language. The app will need to restart for the change to take full effect.

Is my practice data private?

Yes! All your data is stored locally on your device only. VirtuoPlan never uploads your data to servers or shares it with third parties.

Can I pause practice sessions?

Yes! During a practice session, you can pause and resume as needed. The app tracks only active practice time, excluding paused periods.

How do notifications work?

When you create a practice plan, VirtuoPlan schedules notifications 30 minutes before each session. Make sure to enable notifications in iOS Settings for the app.

What are widgets?

Widgets show your today's practice schedule on your home screen. Add the VirtuoPlan widget to see upcoming sessions at a glance without opening the app.

Can I delete practice sessions?

Yes, you can delete individual sessions by swiping left on them or using the context menu. Note that deleting a session will also remove its practice time from your instrument's total.

How do I reset all my data?

Go to Settings ‚Üí Reset All Data. This will permanently delete all instruments, practice plans, and sessions. This action cannot be undone.
